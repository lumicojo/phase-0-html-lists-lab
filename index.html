• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. first Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. second Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. third Add cheese on top of bread
  4. forth Cover with second slice of bread
  5. fifth Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
